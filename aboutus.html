Our Story

BAQATI was founded with a simple mission: to bring the beauty and joy of fresh flowers to every home and occasion. Located in the heart of Muscat, we have been serving our community with premium quality flowers and exceptional service.

We believe that flowers are more than just decorations - they are expressions of love, celebration, sympathy, and joy. Each bouquet we create is carefully crafted to convey the perfect message for your special moments.

Our team of experienced florists work tirelessly to source the freshest blooms from local and international growers, ensuring that every arrangement meets our high standards of quality and beauty.
